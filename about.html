About Us

Friendly Tour pertama dibuat untuk memenuhi tugas yang akhirnya dikembangkan oleh sekelompok anak muda untuk membuat usaha yang menghasilkan. Berawal dari hobi travelling bersama ke berbagai tempat membuat mereka setuju untuk membuat usaha bersama yang sekaligus bisa menyalurkan hobi mereka.

With the help of expert local guides, travelers can gain insider access to their destination with customized, unique tours that address their interests and provide a richer travel experience. Make the most of your time on shore with a customized shore excursion, book a historical tour of Rome, live life like a local in Bangkok. The possibilities of experiences with Viator Private Tour Guides are limited only by your own imagination.

Founded in 2008, Viator Private Tour Guides (formerly 'OurExplorer') was acquired by Viator, Inc. in 2010 and is part of the Viator, Inc. family of tours and things to do websites.
